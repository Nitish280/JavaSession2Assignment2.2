Compiler:
1.It takes the whole source code at once.
2.It Checks the whole program at once
2.Program need not to compiled every time.
3.Error are displayed after whole program is checked.

Interpreter:
1.It takes the single instruction at once.
2.It checks the instruction one by one.
2.Every time higher level program is converted into lower level program.
3.Error are displayed after every instruction.
